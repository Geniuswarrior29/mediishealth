💳 Ayushman Card Support

Navigate Your Healthcare Benefits with Ease

The Ayushman Bharat scheme is a revolutionary step towards universal health coverage, but navigating the eligibility criteria and hospital networks can be confusing. We act as your dedicated desk for all Ayushman Card queries.

We help you understand your benefits, check your eligibility, and connect you with the right empanelled hospitals for free treatments and surgeries.

  • Instant eligibility and KYC status checks.
  • Guidance on covered diseases and treatment packages.
  • Assistance in finding top-rated empanelled hospitals nearby.
  • Step-by-step help with the admission process under the scheme.

  • Second opinions on recommended surgeries.

🤝 CM Fund Assistance

Financial Guidance for Crucial Treatments

Financial constraints should never stand in the way of life-saving medical treatments. For underprivileged patients requiring expensive surgeries or chronic care, the Chief Minister's Relief Fund provides vital support.

Our team guides you through the complex application process, ensuring you have the correct documentation, estimates, and forms required to successfully apply for government health funds.

  • Detailed checklist of required documents (Income certificate, Aadhaar, etc.).
  • Assistance in obtaining proper medical estimates from authorized hospitals.
  • Step-by-step guidance on filling and submitting the application.
  • Follow-up strategies to track the status of your application.
